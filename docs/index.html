Everything You Need to Plan Your Finances

📈

Day-by-Day Balance Projection

See exactly where your balance will be on any future date. The projection engine simulates your income and expenses with 7 recurrence intervals.

🏦

Multi-Account Management

Track checking, savings, and more. Set up transfers between accounts — an expense in one automatically appears as income in the other.

✅

Actual vs. Forecast

Record what you actually spent and link it to forecasts. The projection adjusts automatically — forecast where you don't have actuals, reality where you do.

📊

5 Interactive Chart Views

Projection, Spending by Category, Income vs Expenses, Cash Flow, and Expense Trends. Expand any chart to fullscreen with zoom and controls.

🔍

Fullscreen Zoom

Expand any chart to full screen. Click two points to zoom into a date range, or use the sliders. Toggle between area/line, grouped/stacked, and more.

📄

Spreadsheet Exchange

Export to Excel, edit in your favorite spreadsheet app, import back. Professional formatting with instructions, validation, and color-coded sheets.

🏷

Category System

Organize expenses with custom categories, colors, and descriptions. Charts color-code by category. Rename and it updates everywhere.

💰

Variable Pricing

Track expenses that change over time — rent increases, subscription changes. Add price adjustments with dates and the projection stays accurate.

🤔

What-If Analysis

Toggle any income or expense on/off to instantly see how it affects your future balance. Plan before you commit.

Up and Running in 60 Seconds

Just Docker and three commands.

# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/Dreddsparc/Budget-Tracker.git
cd Budget-Tracker

# Set up environment
cp .env.example .env

# Launch everything
make dev
  • 1
    Docker builds everything — database, server, and client containers start automatically with hot reload.
  • 2
    Open localhost:5173 — set your starting balance, add income and expenses.
  • 3
    See your future — the projection chart shows your balance trajectory instantly. Toggle items for what-if analysis.
